This is the Local Loveland Bakery's website! We are a business set in Loveland Ohio that mainly produced bread for customers to request, buy, and enjoy! Currently run by a small group of twenty employees, we are all very dedicated to make your purchase worth while at our establishment.
We make many different types of bread, and even take requests! For a general catalog with some information on each thing we've made before, check the catalog page! If you are looking to make a request, you can find our location on its page or send us a message through the contact page.
We are a small business that is not part of a chain, and we do not have delivery! We operate mainly with in person help, so please keep that in mind! Additionally, we encourage you to support as many local sellers as you can, because they really need it. Its very important to consider the people you live around and how you can help everyone in your community get better! Thank you so much for visiting our website! We hope you have a great day and swing by soon!
