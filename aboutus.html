Help with Navigating the Website

If you were unable to find this information other places, here is a little bit of info regarding how we operate: We are open 8 - 10 all days but Sunday, our off day, you can find our location in the 'location' page, and along with it, a generalized table of how long it'll take us to make certain things. Additionally, if you want to contact us, go to the 'contact' page and fill out the form and we will get in touch! If you wish to see some general things we make, whether its to learn a bit more about bread or to spark your brain to think about what you'd like to order, the 'catalog' page has plenty to show you!
